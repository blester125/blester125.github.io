Constrained Decoding for Computationally Efficient Named Entity Recognition Taggers

SustaiNLP Workshop

November 2020

A talk about how constrained decoding, a way to make sure taggers do not output an illegal series of tokens, can be used instead of a CRF as the final output of a BiLSTM tagger. The differences in model performance are statistically insignificant and constrained decoding yields a x2 speedup for training.
