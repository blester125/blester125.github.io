Exponential Decay

\[ \begin{aligned} \textrm{decay rate} &= r \\ \textrm{decay step} &= s \\ \textrm{initial learning rate} &= \eta \\ \textrm{current step} &= x \\ \textrm{learning rate} &= y \\ y &= \eta * r ^ {\frac{x}{s}} \\ \frac{y}{\eta} &= r^{\frac{x}{s}} \\ \log \frac{y}{\eta} &= \log r^{\frac{x}{s}} \\ \log \frac{y}{\eta} &= \frac{x}{s} \log r \\ \frac{\log \frac{y}{\eta}}{\log{r}} &= \frac{x}{s} \\ \frac{\log \frac{y}{\eta}}{\log{r}} * s &= x \\ \end{aligned} \]

Inverse Time Decay

\[ \begin{aligned} \textrm{decay rate} &= r \\ \textrm{decay step} &= s \\ \textrm{initial learning rate} &= \eta \\ \textrm{current step} &= x \\ \textrm{learning rate} &= y \\ y &= \frac{\eta}{1.0 + r * \frac{x}{s}} \\ \frac{\eta}{y} &= 1.0 + r * \frac{x}{s} \\ \frac{\eta}{y} - 1.0 &= r * \frac{x}{s} \\ \frac{\frac{lr}{y} - 1.0}{r} &= \frac{x}{s} \\ \frac{\frac{lr}{y} - 1.0}{r} * s &= x \\ \end{aligned} \]
